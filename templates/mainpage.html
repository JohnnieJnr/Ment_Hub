About

'If I have seen further, it is by standing on ye sholders of Giants.' - Isaac Newton

Bernard of Chartres used to say that we (the Moderns) are like dwarves perched on the shoulders of giants (the Ancients), and thus we are able to see more and farther than the latter. And this is not at all because of the acuteness of our sight or the stature of our body, but because we are carried aloft and elevated by a magnitude of giants.
If you haven't guessed until now, the 'Moderns' refers to the youth and the Ancients are the experienced.
The Mentorship Hub seeks to create a partnership between the former and the latter. We here acknowledge that education is not merely the training of the mind to think along a certain line. Expanded further, it is the acquisition of knowledge, skills set, and attitude amongst other things. What comes to a lot of people's minds at the mention of the word education, however, is 'that arduous task of studying just to pass tests'. It is rather unfortunate that the full essence of education remains largely unknown. This explains why students graduate school with the intention of getting a job only to survive and not for the love it. They complain at the end of the day that the jobs are exhaustive and boring. There's no excitement, no success at it.
At Mentorship Hub, the aura is one of possibilities and a love for daily activities. In seeking to conquer the whims of mediocrity, we present to the growing world a vast array of people who have excelled in their various fields of endeavour. The goal is to establish a rapport between the young individuals about to be launched into as well as those already in the working world and in want of success of some kind. Everybody needs mentoring, we have concluded, and the world will definitely be a better place should paths be lit up and the knowledge of destinations established in people's minds.

Mentorship Hub is also a project that is geared towards achieving two of the Sustainable Development Goals; that of Quality Education and Partnerships to Achieve Goals. Great right? This simply means we're not limited to the establishment of rapports in certain professions. We describe ourselves as unlimited as we know everyone needs the education and we know that everyone impacts the world in their own unique ways.
